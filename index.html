About the awesome birthday Druidess

The druidess is a powerful and kind creature with wizard-like powers who uses nature to protect and help creatures. Even though, she is only 9 human years old, in Druid form she is 900 years old. Her favorite companion to help her is an ancient brown laborador guardian which looks cute but is very tough. Even though, she is very strong against many classes, her main weakness is against little brother barbarian class. Little brother barbarian class often casts "Mighty irritation" which causes confusion to the druidess.
